Timeless Elegance, One Strand  at a Time

— We strive to provide luxurious hair extensions that exude refinement, allowing You to express your unique style and enhance your natural beauty with an air of opulence.

Candius Diallo is a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for redefining the standards of luxury in the hair care and salon industry. With a relentless commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, She has earned a distinguished reputation as a trailblazer in the field.

Our mission at La Verita Hair is to establish ourselves as the leading human hair processing and custom hair blending factory in Africa by selling the highest quality and widest variety of hair textures directly from Nigeria to clients and customers with excellent customer satisfaction.

We are going into the hearts, minds, homes and heads of the whole of Nigeria. My NGO 'Let's Give Back' along with The La Verita Foundation will grow alongside La Verita Hair, adding value to both the Nigerian economy and communities throughout. La Verita’s School of Cosmetology will train and license individuals who are interested in learning the truth about hair and how to care for it.
